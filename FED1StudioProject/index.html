About Us

< Fine dining is more than simply preparing food. From learning how to pick
the best cuts of meats and select the freshest fruits and vegetables to
understanding the eye pleasing presentation of food, it takes careful
thought and preperation to make dining a memorable event.
The World Cuisine Institue will take you from home kitchen to world class dining.

Why Choose Us

At the World Culinary Institue we prepare students to experience the world of flavors and food preparation. Our students will be exposed to foods from across the globe, from Asia to Italy our students will learn the secrets of each culture's ability to create memorable dining event through the use of food. They will learn not just the importance of food preperation, but the importance of creating the ideal visual presentation of the meal to spark excitement about dining.

Our graduates have been eagerly sought after by restaurants worldwide. Over the years, a culinary graduate will not only be able to have the opportunities to work globally, they will have learned the skills to begin them in a journey of owning and managing their own fine dining restaurant if they so chose. We at the World Cuisine Institue take great pride in knowing our Chef's continue to bring joy to people over the presentation of meals.
